Help

Selecting Locations:


Method 1: Click on a state to view locations in and around the vicinity ? Click on a marker for an overview of the location ? Click on "More Info" for a detailed description of Points of Interest, Transportation and Housing Options, and other Activities around the area

Method 2: Select a location from the drop down menu for a detailed description of Points of Interest, Transportation and Housing Options, and other Activities around the area

Closing the Information Modal:


Click on "Close", Press "Esc" key, or click outside of the Modal

Comparing Locations:


Open up a location ? Click "Compare this location" in bottom-left corner ? Repeat for another location ? Click "Compare Locations" below the map ? View a side-by-side comparison of each of the selected locations

You can compare up to 3 locations, and must select at least 2 locations to compare
